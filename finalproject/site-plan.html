Individual Project Site Plan

IntelligentFlow

Site Name

IntelligentFlow

Reasoning: The name was chosen to directly communicate the site's purpose. "Intelligent" refers to the AI-powered assistants and smart solutions offered, while "Flow" relates to the automation of workflows and business processes. It's professional, modern, and descriptive of the services.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the IntelligentFlow website is to serve as a primary marketing and lead-generation tool for a B2B consultancy specializing in business process automation and AI-powered customer service bots. The site will educate potential clients on the benefits of automation, detail the specific services offered, and provide clear calls-to-action for scheduling a free consultation, thereby converting visitors into qualified leads.

Scenarios

These are questions that a potential visitor might have, which the website content aims to answer:

  • "My administrative team is spending too much time manually transferring data between our spreadsheet and our CRM. Can this process be automated to reduce errors and free up their time?"
  • "We receive a high volume of repetitive questions through our website's chat. Can we implement a bot to handle these common queries 24/7, allowing our support staff to focus on more complex issues?"

Color Schema

The color schema is designed to be professional, trustworthy, and modern, reflecting the technological nature of the business.

Primary: #005A9C

Used for headings, primary buttons, icons, and key accents to draw attention and establish brand identity.

Secondary: #F4F8FB

A light, off-white blue used for section backgrounds to create a clean, subtle separation between content areas.

Text: #333333

A dark gray chosen for all body text to ensure high readability and a softer feel than pure black.

Typography

A single, clean sans-serif font family is used across the site to maintain a modern and professional appearance, ensuring consistency and readability.

Primary Font: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif
